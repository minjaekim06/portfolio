About Me

My long-term career path is shaped by an interest in both the practice of medicine and biomedical engineering. I intend to pursue medical school and become a practicing physician, while remaining closely connected to the fields of biomedical engineering and biorobotics to improve areas of diagnostic medicine, treatment, and patient care. The purpose of this portfolio is to highlight the academic, technical, and leadership experiences that demonstrate my potential in this interdisciplinary trajectory, and to show how my engineering foundation supports my aspirations in medicine.

Through this portfolio, I aim to illustrate not only what I have accomplished so far, but also how each experience prepares me for an interdisciplinary career at the intersection of medicine, biomedical engineering, and robotics. I hope to demonstrate a clear path toward becoming a physician who designs, influences, and improves the technologies that shape patient diagnostics, treatment, and care.
